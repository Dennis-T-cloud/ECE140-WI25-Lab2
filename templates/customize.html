    • Product Idea

    Automatic folding equipment

    Product Description

    A machine that can help people automatically dispose of their clothes, maybe. Help people fold and dry their clothes. Free people from the slavery of washing machines!

    Customer Interview Validation Result

    Questions:

  • 1, How do you feel about folding clothes? Do you enjoy it, or is it a chore?
  • 2, What does your ideal laundry experience look like, from start to finish?
  • 3, If a device existed to fold your clothes automatically, would it make your life easier? Would you invest in it?

    • People's responses (online interview):

    Derek J

    1, Folding clothes, is such a waste of my time.

    2, Honestly, I just want to throw everything in the washer, press a button, and have it come out clean, dry, and already folded.

    3, I’d buy that device, but, what’s the price?

    Abnor Zhenli

    1, A peaceful task.

    2, An ideal experience would integrate smart tech, like an app syncs my laundry preferences to devices that handle everything for me.

    3, No.

    Key takeaways:

    From these brief interviews, I found that most people want a fully automated laundry process. It’s not just folding clothes they dislike—many aren’t fans of washing clothes either. Offering a washing machine that also folds clothes could be a better way. That said, if the cost is too high, people would probably fold their clothes themselves.
